* **User Authentication:** Register and log in to manage your personal health data and notes.
* **Daily Health Tracking:** Record your health metrics including:
    * Date
    * Time Period (Morning, Afternoon, Night)
    * BP Level (in mmHg, e.g., 120/80)
    * Sugar Level (in mg/dL, e.g., 90)
* **Data Management:**
    * Add new health entries.
    * Delete individual health entries.
* **Data Persistence:** All health entries and sticky notes are saved locally in your browser, tied to your user account.
* **Data Export:** Export your complete health data into an Excel (.xlsx) file.
* **Movable Sticky Notes:**
    * Add custom notes that appear as interactive sticky notes.
    * Drag and reposition notes anywhere on the screen.
    * Note positions and content persist across sessions and refreshes, specific to your user account.
* **Sleek Minimal Design:** A clean, modern, and user-friendly interface.
* **Responsive Layout:** Adapts for optimal viewing on various screen sizes (desktop, tablet, mobile).
* **Custom Modals:** Utilizes custom-designed modals for alerts and confirmations.
* **Input Validation:** Basic validation for health input fields (e.g., BP format, sugar level range).

**Important Security Notice:** Please remember that this application stores all user data (including usernames, passwords, health data, and notes) in plain text within your browser's local storage. It is designed purely for demonstration and learning purposes.
